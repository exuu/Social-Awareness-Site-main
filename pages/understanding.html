Understanding:
Understanding others does not mean that you have to agree with their feelings or point of view. Instead, it means that you recognise their point of view, and accept that it is different from yours. You may still have to do difficult things that others do not agree with. Here are some ways to become more understanding:
  • Be curious about the person you want to be more understanding to
  • Don't always assume things
  • Listen well and pick up emotional cues
    • Show sensetivity and understand their persepctives
  • Helping other appropriately
