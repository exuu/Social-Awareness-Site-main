Listening:
Listening is the skill to accurately recieve and interpret messages in communicating with someone. Listening is the most important part in social awareness, because without it, what other people mean are misunderstood and often leads to arguments. Sometimes, it is better if we just listen and not say anything at all, but if we just listen and not show them that we are listening, they might get irritated or annoyed and they won't tell you anything. Here are some ways to improve your listening skill:
  • Maintain Eye Contact with your speaker
  • Don't interrupt and adjusting your tone
  • Pay attention to nonverbal cues
  • Limiting judgments
